# Whale Wallet Tracker

This skill focuses on 'Following the Money' on-chain and off-chain to predict major market shifts.

## Instructions
1. Scrape block-explorers and institutional filings (13F).
2. Identify 'Accumulation' clusters before they are public knowledge.
3. Alert the user when a 'Whale' exits a major position in commodities or crypto.
