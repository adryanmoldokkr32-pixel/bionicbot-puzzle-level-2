# Holographic Landing Pages

This skill creates 'Adaptive Interfaces' that change their layout, copy, and colors based on a user's psychographic profile.

## Instructions
1. Use IP and browser headers to detect user context.
2. Morph the 'Hero' section to match the user's likely intent (Analytical vs Emotional).
3. Automatically switch between Romanian and English based on location.

## Examples
- "Redesign our landing page so it shows 'Security' to EU users and 'Profit' to US users."
